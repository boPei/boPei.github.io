Hi, I am Bo Pei

I am a Ph.D. candidate of Educational Technology in School of Teaching & Learning at University of Florida.

My research interests lie at Learning Analytics, Machine Learning, and Fair AI in education. Currently, I pursuit equitable education algorithmically through exploring approaches to detect and mitigate the unintentional discrimination that the Machine Learning algorithms may introduce in education while used to support the teaching and learning practices. I also investigate ways to ravel how students make sense of the visual representations to enhance their understandings about abstract phenomena in science learning processes. I explicitly examine students' Evidence-based Reasoning quality by employing image processing algorithms to quantify the associations between visual evidence on images and understanding level of the phenomena.

Image Processing supports better understandings of Scientific Reasoning


Project Description

Examining students’ scientific argumentations in correlation with the presence of a certain visual artifact (i.e., water) on the images. Image processing approaches were employed to quantify the water regions shown in the images. Statistical analysis indicated that the size of the water regions in the images has a significant correlation with students’ understanding of the water cycling process.

Machine Learning approaches detecting students' online learning patterns


Project Description

Using machine learning models extract the underlying learning patterns from students' learning activities and forum posts. By combining with the motivation theories, achievement emotions and engagement theories, we build models that predict student learning performance at an early stage while offering actionable insights for individualized interventions.

Bibliometric analysis for multimodal learning analytics


Project Description

Investigating the potentials of incorporating multimodal learning data (i.e., video data, psychological data, etc.) in prediction students’ learning performance by conducting a systematic bibliometric analysis of the research in this field. We discovered that topics related using machine learning approaches to analyze students’ learning in collaborative learning environments have been receive great popularity in recent years.

Fair AI to ensure education equity


Project Description

(1) Exploring approaches for AI algorithms to generate fair predictions across different student groups while ensuring predictive accuracy in educational settings；（2）Implementing platforms that incorporate techniques to visualize the decision-making processes of AI algorithms to increase the prediction transparency.
